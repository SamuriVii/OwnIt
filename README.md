## The Vision: Own Your Life
**OwnIt** is more than just a tool; it's a personal operating system designed to bring order to the chaos of daily life. The goal is to create a unified, high-performance platform to manage everything that matters: **Training, Nutrition, Finances, and Productivity.**

Built with "class and principles," it rejects the "quick and dirty" approach in favor of a robust, production-grade foundation that can grow as life gets more complex.

---

## Technical Foundation (The Boilerplate)
The project starts with a **high-standard Backend Boilerplate**, ensuring that the core of the system is secure, scalable, and maintainable from day one.

### Engineering Standards
* **Domain-Driven Design (DDD):** Logic is organized around life domains, not just database tables.
* **Clean Architecture:** Strict separation between business rules and external frameworks.
* **Modern Python Stack:** Fully asynchronous, strictly typed (mypy), and linted with Ruff.
* **DevOps Ready:** Containerized with Docker and managed by the ultra-fast `uv` package manager.
